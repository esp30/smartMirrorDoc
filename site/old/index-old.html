IoT for Nutrition? What's that all about?

We are consuming ever bigger portions of food on ever larger plates!

Why should we care about the size of our portions? We'll eat more than we should, with all the potential consequences for our health!

In canteens the problem is worse since we don't know the contents/ingredients of each recipe!

IoT for Nutrition plans on using the concept of IoT, of connecting devices installed in a canteen to the Internet, to help users and canteen workers to easier manage and control food portions in each plate, creating a balanced meal, in an easy way.
