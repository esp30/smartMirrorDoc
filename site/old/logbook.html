How do we work?

Each week contains at least 1 team meeting and 1 meeting with the mentors.
More meetings can be done, in person, or using the team's communication platform, Microsoft Teams, but the weekly team and mentors meetings are the most important since it's there we make all the major decisions.

How do we distribute our work?

Each module has at least 1 responsible, who can be helped by several deputies.
The purpose of the responsibles is not to assign all the work related to the module but having someone who knows all the time the current state of the module development.
