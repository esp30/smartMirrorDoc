Why a Pilot Test?

During PEI we are creating a proof-of-concept

During our work in Project of Informatics Engineering, we are trying to prove our system is possible by presenting a working proof-of-concept of a solution to solve the food portions issue.

But we are leaving behind real-world issues

But proving a proof-of-concept means leaving aside considerations about the system's usability on real deployment situations.

How are we planning to solve that?

The implementation of a pilot version of our system at Crasto’s Canteen, under the responsibility of SASUA (Serviços de Ação Social da Universidade de Aveiro), will hopefully lead to a better perspective on the user side regarding the system’s usability and possibly unveil details that require adjustment.
The feedback from both the canteen as well as the users that will have their meal there will be critical in order to progress with the project and ensure that the next steps taken will be on the right direction.
